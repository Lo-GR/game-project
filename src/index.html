Welcome to Planet Escape

You are are a space marine stranded on an unknown planet. Your ship had a major malfunction. You now have to make like Matt Damon and science your way out of this situation

You have 20 days to repair 3 space parts. You will need to collect iron to collect in order to repair those parts. You will also need to buy a welder to make the space parts. Use your days wisely, as you will only be able to do one thing every day.

  • Go on an adventure (returns iron and gold)
  • Go to Space Shop (spend gold to purchase tools)
  • Construct Space Part
